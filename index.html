Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda video game series has been around since 1986. Explore this page to learn more about the prominent games and characters!
Link is the main protagonist of the The Legend of Zelda series. He is the young hero of Hyrule and wielder of the legendary Master Sword.
Zelda is a prominent character in The Legend of Zelda series. She is the princess of Hyrule, and longtime friend of Link.
Ganondorf is considered the antagonist of The Legend of Zelda series. Known as the King of Thieves, and "pure-evil", he seeks the Triforce for himself.
Epona is Link's horse in many games throughout the series. Bonded together since she was a foal, she helps Link travel across Hyrule.
Impa is a royal Sheikah guard, sworn to protect the royal family of Hyrule. She acts as an advisor, friend, and protector to Zelda.
Midna is the ruler of the Twilight Realm. When her realm begins to bleed into Link's the pair team up to restore balance to the kindgom.
Koroks are children of the forest. Protected by the Great Deku Tree, these forest creatures take in and support Link.
Groose is a student at the Skyloft Knight Academy. He is Link's rival, and is jealous of his close relationship with Zelda.
